Apple Inc (AAPL) - Q4 2024 Analysis

Apple reported revenue of $94.9 billion for Q4 2024, representing a 6% year-over-year increase. iPhone revenue came in at $46.2 billion, driven by strong demand for the iPhone 16 Pro models. Services revenue hit a new record at $25.0 billion, growing 12% year-over-year.

Gross margin improved to 46.2%, up from 45.2% in the prior year quarter. The company returned over $29 billion to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases during the quarter.

Key risks include regulatory pressure in the EU regarding App Store policies, increased competition in China from Huawei, and potential supply chain disruptions. The company's AI strategy with Apple Intelligence is still in early stages and has not yet shown material revenue impact.

Analyst consensus price target is $245, with a range of $190 to $300. The stock trades at approximately 32x forward earnings, which is above its 5-year average of 27x.

Recommendation: Hold for existing positions. The premium valuation requires continued execution on services growth and AI integration. New positions should wait for a pullback to the $200-210 range for better risk-reward.
